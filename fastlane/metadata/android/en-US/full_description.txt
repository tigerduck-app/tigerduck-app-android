TigerDuck is an open-source campus assistant app for National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (NTUST) students.

It brings together scattered campus services into a single native Android experience:

- Sync assignments from Moodle with deadline notifications and reminders
- Course schedule sync from the selection system with an interactive time-machine timeline
- Integrated campus calendar combining school events and Moodle deadlines
- Customizable themes, notification preferences, and display settings

Built by students, for students.
